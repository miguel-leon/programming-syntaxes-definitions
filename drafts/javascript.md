# Open and Close Braces RegExp Recursive Form

- Depth 1
```regexp
{(?:|[^}])*}
```

- Depth 2
```regexp
{(?:{(?:|[^}])*}|[^}])*}
```

- Depth 4
```regexp
{(?:{(?:{(?:{(?:|[^}])*}|[^}])*}|[^}])*}|[^}])*}
```

- Depth 8
```regexp
{(?:{(?:{(?:{(?:{(?:{(?:{(?:{(?:|[^}])*}|[^}])*}|[^}])*}|[^}])*}|[^}])*}|[^}])*}|[^}])*}|[^}])*}
```
